Bolognese Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 10 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1/2 cup red wine (optional)

Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add chopped onion and minced garlic, saut√© until soft.
  3. Add ground beef, cook until browned.
  4. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, oregano, salt, and pepper.
  5. If using, add red wine and simmer for 30 minutes.
  6. Serve over pasta or as desired.
