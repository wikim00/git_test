Carbonara Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 lb spaghetti
  • 4 oz pancetta or guanciale, diced
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions until al dente.
  2. In a skillet, cook pancetta or guanciale over medium heat until crispy.
  3. In a bowl, whisk together eggs and Parmesan cheese.
  4. Add minced garlic to the skillet and saut√© for 1 minute.
  5. Drain spaghetti and add it to the skillet with pancetta.
  6. Remove from heat and quickly stir in egg mixture, mixing well.
  7. Season with salt and pepper, and garnish with parsley if desired.
